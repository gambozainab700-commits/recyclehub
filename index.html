About Us

EcoValue Recyclers Hub connects communities, local waste pickers, and farmers to create sustainable recycling solutions. We equip waste pickers with tools, provide training, and convert organic and plastic waste into valuable products.

Learn More

Our Services

Waste Pickup

Book pickups from markets and communities. Our trained pickers collect responsibly and efficiently.

Waste Processing

Organic waste is converted into bio-fertilizer while plastics are sorted and recycled for reuse.

Farmer Solutions

We provide affordable bio-fertilizer to farmers and promote sustainable agriculture practices.

Impact

  • Support local waste pickers and improve livelihoods
  • Reduce pollution and landfill waste
  • Track tons of waste collected and recycled
  • Provide transparency and measurable community impact
